## 2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in three ways:

### 2.1 Information you provide

- **Account & profile** — name, email address, phone number, gender, date of
  birth, profile photo, preferred name.
- **Employment information** — payroll ID, department, work authorization
  type, hire/termination dates, pay structure, overtime threshold, workers'
  compensation code, housing assignment, manager relationships.
- **Operational data** — schedules, harvest weights, food-safety checklist
  responses, pack-line productivity, inventory orders, sales orders, customer
  records, training records, time-off and travel requests, performance
  reviews, disciplinary records.
- **Authentication** — password (stored hashed and salted) or Google OAuth
  identity from Google sign-in.

### 2.2 Information generated by use

- **Activity logs** — actions you take in the Service (record creation,
  edits, approvals).
- **Audit trail** — `created_by`, `updated_by`, and timestamps on records.
- **AI-assist transcripts** — prompts and responses when you use AI form
  assistance or the chat assistant (only when enabled).

### 2.3 Information from third parties

- **Google** — your name and email address when you sign in with Google.
- **Intuit** — QuickBooks customer, invoice, and payment data when an
  organization has authorized the QuickBooks Online integration.
- **SPS Commerce** — EDI documents (purchase orders, shipping notices,
  invoices) when an organization has authorized the SPS integration.

We do **not** intentionally collect special categories of personal data
(racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, biometric
data, etc.) other than the limited employment-related fields necessary for
HR operations (gender, date of birth) which are kept under the same access
controls as the rest of the HR record.

## 3. How We Use Information

We process personal information to:

- (a) operate, maintain, and secure the Service;
- (b) authenticate users and route them to the correct organization and
  modules per their role-based access (RBAC) configuration;
- (c) compute payroll allocations, schedules, harvest yields, sales
  fulfillment, food-safety compliance, and other ERP outputs from the data
  you provide;
- (d) integrate with third-party services you have authorized (QuickBooks
  Online, Google OAuth, SPS Commerce);
- (e) provide AI-assisted features (form assist, chat) when enabled by the
  Customer;
- (f) detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, security incidents, and
  legal violations;
- (g) comply with legal obligations including FSMA traceability, payroll
  reporting, and tax reporting.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use personal information for
advertising or behavioral profiling.

## 5. Cookies & Local Storage

The Service uses session cookies and browser local storage solely to keep you
signed in, remember your selected organization and theme preference, and
maintain the in-memory state of forms you are editing. We do not use
advertising or analytics cookies.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain Customer Data for as long as the Customer maintains an active
relationship with Hawaii Farming and as required by applicable law (for
example, payroll and food-safety records have multi-year retention
requirements under federal and state regulations).

When a record is logically deleted in the Service it is marked
`is_deleted = true` and excluded from active queries; the underlying row may
be retained in encrypted backups for up to **35 days** before purge.

After Customer relationship ends, we will purge Customer Data from active
systems within **90 days** unless retention is required by law.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on the jurisdiction in which you reside, you may have the right to:

- access the personal information we hold about you;
- request correction of inaccurate information;
- request deletion of personal information (subject to retention obligations);
- object to or restrict certain processing;
- data portability — receive a machine-readable copy of your data;
- withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact **privacy@hawaiifarming.com**. We
will respond within the time required by applicable law (typically 30 days).

For employees, certain rights may be exercised through your HR contact rather
than directly through the Service.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards
including:

- TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit;
- AES-256 encryption at rest (Supabase / Google Cloud managed);
- Role-based access control (RBAC) enforced at the database row level via
  PostgreSQL Row-Level Security policies, scoped by organization membership;
- Audit logging of access to sensitive data;
- Regular review of third-party access and security posture.

No method of transmission over the Internet or storage is 100% secure. While
we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly
collect personal information from children. If you believe we have
inadvertently collected information from a child, contact us so we can
delete it.

## 10. International Transfers

Our infrastructure is hosted in the United States. If you access the Service
from outside the United States, your personal information will be transferred
to and processed in the United States. By using the Service you consent to
the transfer.
